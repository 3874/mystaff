Frequently Asked Questions

Go to "My Interests" page and click the "+" button to add a new staff member. Fill in the required information including adapter configuration and test your setup.

We support HTTP, OpenAI, and Gemini adapters. Each adapter type has its own configuration requirements. HTTP adapters are for custom APIs, while OpenAI and Gemini are for their respective AI services.

When creating or editing a staff member, scroll down to the "Test Configuration" section. For database resources, the test view will load automatically. For other resources, enter a test prompt and click the "Test" button.

Visit the "Credentials" page from the sidebar menu. There you can manage API keys, tokens, and other authentication credentials for your AI services.
