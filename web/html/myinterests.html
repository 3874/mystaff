My Interests

This page manages your "Interns," which are AI agents you have created or registered.

  • My Interests: These are AI agents that are fully registered and ready to be used. You can chat with them or view their profiles.
  • DIY Staffs: These are configurations for AI agents that you have started creating but have not yet finalized. You can continue building them from here.
  • Build Button: Click the "Build" button to open a form where you can create a new AI staff from scratch. You can define its properties, adapter for connecting to an AI model (like OpenAI or Gemini), and test its responses before saving.
